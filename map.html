Make Music Day is open to participation from all musicians and bands of any level of experience, genre or age... register to find a great location to play and take part in the celebration of music!

Make Music Day takes place all over the world at places ranging from porches to rooftops, storefronts to street corners, and parks to stages and venues. Sign up to add your location to the mix and find a band you love to come perform at your own venue!

Help-giving volunteers make the rocking world go round... sign up to volunteer to help coordinate this incredible grassroots effort in your city!
